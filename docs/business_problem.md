# Customer Churn Prediction

## Problem Statement

Telecommunication companies lose customers every month because some customers stop using their services and switch to competitors. Customer churn reduces revenue, increases customer acquisition costs, and negatively affects business growth. The company wants to identify customers who are likely to churn so it can take preventive actions before they leave.

---

## Business Objective

Develop a machine learning model that predicts whether a customer is likely to churn based on historical customer information such as demographics, subscription details, billing information, and service usage.

The prediction will help the company identify high-risk customers and implement retention strategies such as discounts, personalized offers, or improved customer support.

---

## Success Criteria

- Build a reliable churn prediction model.
- Achieve strong classification performance using metrics such as Precision, Recall, F1-score, and ROC-AUC.
- Identify the most important factors influencing customer churn.
- Enable business teams to proactively target customers who are likely to leave.

---

## Expected Business Impact

A successful churn prediction system can:

- Reduce customer churn.
- Increase customer retention.
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Increase long-term revenue.
- Reduce marketing costs by focusing retention efforts on high-risk customers.
- Support better business decision-making through data-driven insights.
